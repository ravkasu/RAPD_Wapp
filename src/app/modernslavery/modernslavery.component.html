Modern Slavery

This statement is issued in accordance with Section 54 of the Modern Slavery Act 2015, outlining the measures undertaken by RAPD Services Limited to prevent any instances of modern slavery or human trafficking within our business or supply chain.

Modern slavery encompasses servitude, forced labor, human trafficking, and slavery. RAPD maintains a steadfast commitment to a zero-tolerance approach against all forms of modern slavery. We pledge to conduct all business dealings with the highest ethical standards, integrity, and transparency. We have implemented robust systems and controls to prevent modern slavery within both our operations and supply chain.

Our Operations:

RAPD Services Limited is a dedicated firm specializing in IT and IT Security. Additionally, we manage Project and Resource Solutions. With a presence in India and an offices within the United Kingdom, we operate with a team with skilled professionals..

High-Risk Assessment:

Given that our primary business revolves around the provision of recruitment services for IT professionals, we do not consider ourselves to operate in a sector with a particularly high risk of labor exploitation or modern slavery compared to sectors such as agriculture, retail, or manufacturing. We regularly assess and review this evaluation.

Our Policies:

We maintain a range of internal policies to ensure ethical and transparent business conduct. These include:

Recruitment Policy: We rigorously implement a recruitment policy that includes eligibility checks for the right to work in the UK for all employees and candidates, ensuring protection against human trafficking and forced labor. We exclusively engage with Umbrella Companies accredited by FCSA or Professional Passport to adhere to the Home Office Right to Work Checklist.

Whistleblowing Policy: A whistleblowing policy enables all employees to report concerns about the treatment of colleagues or practices within our business or supply chain without fear of retaliation.

Code of Conduct: RAPD strictly adheres to a Code of Conduct that aligns with our values as an organization and outlines our expectations for employee and supplier behavior.

Our Suppliers:

We perform due diligence on all our suppliers, requiring them to comply with UK Government Legal Requirements regarding Modern Slavery. Our suppliers affirm that:



  • . They take steps to eliminate modern slavery within their organizations.
  • . They hold their own suppliers accountable for modern slavery.
  • . They provide fair wages to their employees, at least in line with the national minimum wage.
  • . They offer protection to whistleblowers.
  • . Any discovery of modern slavery instances could lead to contract termination.

Training & Performance Indicators:

Considering our assessment of limited exposure to modern slavery risk and our existing due diligence processes, we have not introduced specific training programs or key performance indicators related to modern slavery and human trafficking for our UK employees during the previous financial year. However, RAPD encourages our staff to access the Government's Modern Slavery Training Resource Page for additional information.
