Our Privacy Policy

This document outlines how we handle your privacy on Our Website. It is presented by Rapd Service Limited and is designed to safeguard the privacy of those who use our website.

What This Policy Covers

This policy addresses various aspects of user privacy, defining the responsibilities and requirements for users, our website, and its owners. It also provides insights into how we process, store, and protect user data and information.

The Website

Our commitment to your privacy is proactive. We have taken every necessary measure to protect your privacy during your visit. Our website complies with all UK national laws and regulations concerning user privacy.

Use of Cookies

To enhance your experience, we use cookies on our website. We've implemented a cookie control system, enabling you to grant or deny permission for cookies on your device during your initial visit. This adheres to recent legal requirements that mandate user consent for storing or accessing files such as cookies. Cookies are small files stored on your device that record, save, and store information about your interactions and website usage. This enables us to offer you a personalized experience. Users who wish to restrict or block cookies from our website can adjust their browser's security settings accordingly.

Tracking Software

We employ tracking software, courtesy of Google Analytics, to gain insights into how our visitors use our website. While this software uses cookies to track user activity, it doesn't collect personal information. For detailed information, please review Google's privacy policy. When our website engages in referral programs, sponsored links, or advertisements, external vendors may place cookies on your device for conversion and referral tracking. These cookies typically expire after 30 days, with no personal information collected.

Contact & Communication

Users contacting our website do so voluntarily, providing any requested personal details at their discretion. We handle your personal information with utmost privacy and security in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998. This information may be used to provide you with further details about our products and services or to respond to your queries. If you consented during the submission process, your details may also be used to subscribe you to our email newsletter. Rest assured, we never share your details with third parties.

Email Newsletter

Our website may operate an email newsletter program to inform subscribers about our products and services. Users can subscribe through an online automated process or by prior written agreement. All subscription-related personal details are securely held in compliance with UK Spam Laws and the Data Protection Act 1998. Email marketing campaigns may contain tracking facilities to analyze and refine future campaigns, based on subscriber activity. Subscribers can opt out at any time.

External Links

While we strive to include quality and secure external links, please exercise caution when clicking on such links. This website and its owners cannot guarantee the content of externally linked websites.

Social Media Platforms

When engaging with us through social media, be sure to adhere to the terms and conditions and privacy policies of the respective platforms. For sensitive discussions, we recommend contacting us through primary channels like phone or email. Our website may feature social sharing buttons for your convenience. Exercise discretion when using these buttons.

Social media platforms may shorten URLs. Use good judgment when clicking on shortened links, as our website and its owners cannot be held responsible for any implications.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Each version applies to information collected while it was in place. Material changes will be notified by posting the new Privacy Policy on our site. We encourage regular review of this policy for updates. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
